Successful Alumni Stories

Emmanuel Malana

Baliuag University had provided me with the tools and skills for a life-changing opportunity of becoming a multi-awarded teacher-leader in the United States. My education from the Mathematics Department of the College of Education and the training and exposure I had as President of the College of Education and the BU Student Council became my tools to becoming an outstanding teacher-leader in the United States.

Yusoph Maute

It’s been exactly 10 years since I graduated from Baliuag University, and to this day, I am still grateful for the training and experiences I got from BU which prepared me well for the corporate world and opened so many opportunities for me. BU trained me to be an analytical and critical thinker which is very important in my profession. The learnings during my stint as a student leader at BU has also been invaluable for the roles I play at my current work and at various professional organizations I am part of.

Rowhen Roque

With BU’s extensive training and comprehensive nursing curriculum, not only was I able to meet the standards of my home country, but I could sell myself in the competitive world overseas. My Alma Mater cultivated in me that amazing spirit to genuinely provide quality service with humanitarian aplomb wherever I may be. Being dedicated to my craft and continuously striving to be the best version of myself, as nurse and human-being, are virtues and values I will forever treasure and owe from my beloved University.

Regina Parungao

Baliuag University has lived up to its academic reputation and more. Through the help of this institution, I have found opportunities for personal growth and development, not only academically but also about life outside the four corners of a classroom. The university and the professors have showed incredible care and commitment to us students. They have shared their collective wisdom and resources, leading us to find our own meaning and purpose in life.
